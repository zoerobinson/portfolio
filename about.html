About Me


Hi, I'm Zoe


I served in Peace Corps Namibia as a Community Economic Volunteer from April 2017 to June 2019. Throughout my time in Namibia, I focused on youth entrepreneurship at a vocational training school for marginalized populations. I primarily taught entrepreneurship as a core subject, and also conducted outreach trainings in rural areas with my counterpart. During my two years there, I served on the Peace Corps Namibia Media Committee, which afforded me the opportunity to travel all over the country to film different cultures, languages, and volunteer experiences.
